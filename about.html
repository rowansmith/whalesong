Accommodation - Gansbaai - De Kelders

An intimate guest house on top of the rugged Cliffs of De Kelders, offers you whale watching from the comfort of your bed! Stanley & Lainy Carpenter welcome you to their guesthouse Whale Song Lodge overlooking the picturesque Walker Bay with views of the coastline stretching from the white sand dunes of “Die Plaat“ past Hermanus all the way to Cape Point.

Find seafront accommodation on South Africa´s whale coast which offers excellent land-based whale watching, shark cage diving, diverse fynbos, rugged coastal cliffs and deserted beaches; situated only 2 hr from Cape Town just before the fishing village of Gansbaai famous for its Great White Sharks.

Why Whalesong Lodge guest house?

De Kelders is north facing (remember, this is the sunny side in the southern hemisphere) and one of the few places on the Whale Coast of South Africa where you are able to enjoy sundowners while watching the sun set over the Atlantic ocean.

But the biggest reason is (and believe us these southern right whales are big!) there is no place in South Africa were you are able to watch the whales up-close from the shore as you do from the De Kelders cliffs and the accommodation at Whale Song Lodge is on those cliffs only 70m from sea, you can't get closer unless you go for a swim!

Oh, did we mention that we

  • Organise excursions for you: cage-diving with the great white shark, boat based whale watching, guided fynbos hikes, horse riding on secluded beaches.
  • Are 1 ½ hrs drive from Agulhas - do a scenic daytrip along the fynbos road through Elim to the southern tip of Africa.
  • Enjoy a sunset everyday like our ancestors, that first lived here 80 000yrs ago.
  • Located in the newest wine region of South Africa.
  • Serve delicious dinners accompanied by finest Overberg wine.
  • Go all the way to stock what you like for breakfast.
  • Fell asleep in various furniture stores until we selected the correct beds.
  • Disclose the location of secluded bays at our doorstep for save swimming.
  • Direct you to some of the most beautiful beaches the Cape has to offer.
  • Inform you about where to shop in Hermanus, whom to greet in Gansbaai and which fisherman not to believe a single word from.

Suites

Standard Suite

The spacious rooms are thoughtfully yet stylishly decorated with a writing desk and an easy chair. All standard room have some sea view and a small private standing balcony to take in the fresh sea air.

The modern functional en-suite bathrooms are separated from the sleeping area by shoulder-high walls, with under floor heating, separate bath and shower. In the changing area there is an off-the-floor area for your luggage and shelves and hanging space for clothes as well as an electronic safe, hairdryer and make up mirror.

For the warm summer days there is a fan and beach towel for the pool or to take to the beach. There is some filtered water in the flask, fresh flowers and seasonal fruit. In case of a power failure there is a rechargeable Camp light, torch, candles and matches.

Honeymoon Suite

The honeymoon suite is situated at the front of the guesthouse and offers spectacular 180° degree view over Walker Bay. Watch the Southern Right Whales from the comfortable king-size bed or while lying in the spa-bath enjoying a glass of South African bullies.

The spacious room is thoughtfully yet stylishly decorated with a writing desk and two easy chairs in a separate lounge area. The honeymoon suite balcony has a coffee table and 2 chairs.

The modern open plan en-suite bathrooms are separated from the sleeping area by shoulder-high walls, with under floor heating, separate spa bath and shower. In the changing area there is an off-the-floor area for your luggage and shelves and hanging space for clothes as well as an electronic safe, hairdryer and make up mirror.
